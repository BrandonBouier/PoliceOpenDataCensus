This is a census of currently available open datasets about police interactions with citizens in the US, including Use of Force, Officer-Involved Shootings, and Complaints Against Police.

Created by the 2015 Code for America Fellows for Indianapolis www.codeforamerica.org/governments/indianapolis/

Feedback welcome to indy@codeforamerica.org – please let us know about other related data sets to add.
